Wrong. Naughty Dog does something BOLD mid-game: forces you to play as the person Ellie is hunting. You play 10-12 hours as Abby, the "antagonist". And gradually... you start to understand. To empathize. To question everything. ğŸ˜°

**Second playthrough (2022):**
